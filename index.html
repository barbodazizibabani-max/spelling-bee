How to play 🎮

🤫
1

Player 1 picks a word

Type a tricky word and a pronunciation hint — keep it secret!

📱
2

Pass the device

The screen clears automatically. Hand it over to Player 2.

✍️
3

Player 2 spells it

They hear the pronunciation hint and try to spell the word.

🏆
4

Score points

Harder words = more points. Most points at the end wins!

How scoring works 🧮

+1
per letter in the word
+3
per tricky pattern (ph, gh, ie…)
+2
per double letter (ll, ss, tt…)
