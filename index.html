📱 Install on Mobile
No app store needed — works on iPhone & Android!
1
Open in Safari Make sure you're using Safari — it won't work in Chrome or other browsers on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button It's the box with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of the screen.
⬆️
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" Scroll down in the share menu until you see this option and tap it.
➕
4
Tap "Add" Confirm in the top right corner. The bee icon will appear on your home screen!
🐝
1
Open in Chrome Make sure you're using Google Chrome on your Android device.
2
Tap the three dots Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right corner of Chrome.
⋮
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" You might also see "Install App" — tap either one.
➕
4
Tap "Add" Confirm and the bee icon will appear on your home screen!

How to play ↓

How to play 🎮

🤫
1

Player 1 picks a word

Type a tricky word and a pronunciation hint — keep it secret!

📱
2

Pass the device

The screen clears automatically. Hand it over to Player 2.

✍️
3

Player 2 spells it

They hear the pronunciation hint and try to spell the word.

🏆
4

Score points

Harder words = more points. Most points at the end wins!

How scoring works 🧮

+1
per letter in the word
+3
per tricky pattern (ph, gh, ie…)
+2
per double letter (ll, ss, tt…)
